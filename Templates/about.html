Our Story

Founded with a passion for authentic Kashmiri cuisine, our journey began in the heart of Srinagar, where generations of culinary expertise have been passed down through families. We've dedicated ourselves to preserving these traditional recipes while sharing them with food enthusiasts worldwide.

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a celebration of Kashmiri culture through food. Each dish tells a story of our heritage, the lush valleys of Kashmir, and the warmth of our people.

Authentic Flavors

Our cuisine is a harmonious blend of aromatic spices, fresh ingredients, and traditional cooking techniques that have been perfected over centuries. From the fiery warmth of Rogan Josh to the delicate flavors of Yakhni, each dish is a testament to Kashmir's rich culinary heritage.

We source our ingredients directly from local Kashmiri farmers and spice growers, ensuring authenticity and supporting the communities that have cultivated these flavors for generations.

Culinary Excellence

Our master chefs bring decades of experience and a deep understanding of Kashmiri culinary traditions. Trained in the art of Wazwan, the multi-course ceremonial feast, they skillfully balance spices and flavors to create dishes that are both authentic and unforgettable.

We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of quality and authenticity, ensuring that every meal we serve transports you to the beautiful valleys of Kashmir with just one bite.
